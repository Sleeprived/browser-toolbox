Password Vault

A private, offline password manager. Your entries live in one encrypted file that you keep — nothing is uploaded or stored on any server.

🔒 100% local. Encrypted with AES-256-GCM; your master password is stretched with PBKDF2. The file never leaves your device. There is no password reset — if you forget your master password, the data is gone forever, so keep backups of your file.
